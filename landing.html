About this project

In this project, we aspire to serve the crime department by building a site that allows its members to work with higher quality. This project serves the criminology department in:

  • Quick searches, use of time
  • Obtaining information in an easier way
  • Obtaining information that is not available on the internet and backing it up
  • Advanced search tools with AI automations to analize data
  • Research tools to assist crimonlogy research more efficiently

Services we offer



Providing articles and backing up from multiple news sources containing articles that might be removed
The ability to filter articles according to your interests in order to search accurately
Charts and graphs that display organized data statistics to give you a big picture
